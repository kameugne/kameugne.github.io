Biography

I am a Senior Lecturer and Researcher specializing in Applied Mathematics, Constraint Programming, and Combinatorial Optimization. My work combines algorithm design, operations research, and practical optimization for real-world industrial and academic applications.

I collaborate with international research groups across Belgium, France, Canada, and Africa, with contributions in scheduling, resource constraints, and decision-diagram-based optimization. I am currently involved in DDOLib development in collaboration with CETIC and UCLouvain.
